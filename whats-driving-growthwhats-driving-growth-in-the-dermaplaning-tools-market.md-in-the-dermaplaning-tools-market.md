<p>Dermaplaning is the practice of shaving the facial area with a single blade that resembles a blade and is used to remove dead skin cells and peach hair. The dermaplaning equipment appears to be similar to an electric razor. The dermatome consists of a tiny blade that travels back and forth across the skin. It does not cut skin&nbsp;but rather trims off the top layers of skin. Dermaplaning is growing in popularity as a successful exfoliating technique due to the rising demand for non-invasive skincare procedures and the desire for beautiful, youthful-looking skin. Dermaplaning is a desirable alternative for treating issues including uneven skin texture, dullness, and fine facial hair since consumers are drawn to treatments that provide instant results with little downtime. Dermaplaning is becoming an increasingly prominent skincare procedure as a result of its increased visibility and encouraging social media reviews. At-home dermaplaning equipment's accessibility and convenience are key factors propelling the market's growth.</p>
